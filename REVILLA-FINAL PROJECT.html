About R GameLab

R GameLab is a community-driven platform designed specifically for gamers to connect, share, and improve. We noticed that gamers often have to jump between five different apps to find teammates, look up builds, and share their highlights. We built R GameLab to bring it all under one roof.

From tactical shooters like Valorant to immersive RPGs like Genshin Impact, our goal is to provide a "Lab" where strategies are tested and gaming friendships are made.

Help & Support

Having trouble with your account or found a bug? Check our quick links or get in touch with our team.

  • How to post: Click the "Post" bar on the Home feed to share text or attachments.
  • Game Builds: Access the sidebar to see community-shared strategies.

Need direct assistance?

Our team is available to answer questions via email. We usually respond within 24 hours.
